About this tool

OTEC Mapper Neo

Interactive assessment tool for onshore OTEC (Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion) and SWAC (Sea Water Air Conditioning) sites.

Data sources

  • Bathymetry: GEBCO 2025 (15 arc-second grid)
  • Temperature: World Ocean Atlas 2023 (monthly climatology)
  • Currents: GLORYS12V1 reanalysis (Copernicus Marine)

Methodology

Candidate sites are coastal points within the tropics (30N-30S) that have ocean depth of 1000m or more within 30 km. Sites are scored based on distance to deep water, temperature differential between surface and 1000m depth (worst month), pipe route quality, and cross-current intensity.
